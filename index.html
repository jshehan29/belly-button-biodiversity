Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

This dashboard summarizes and displays bacterial species found from swabs of human navels (or belly buttons). The bacterial species found in the study test subjects' navels can be searched by the test subject ID number using the drop down menu. The demographic information, the top ten bacterial species found, the navel wash frequency, and the total bacterial cultures for each test subject are included in this interactive dashboard. The goal of the study is to help the food startup Improbable Beef develop great tasting synthetic beef by finding bacterial species that can synthesize proteins that taste like beef.
